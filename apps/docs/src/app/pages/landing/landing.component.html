It's all just data

Validation, derivations, and conditional logic are plain, serializable data. No callbacks, nothing trapped in your bundle. Your backend can author a form as JSON and send it down, ready to render.

field.ts
  • Async checks, no functions. Declare an HTTP validator. Debounce and cancellation are handled for you.
  • Computed values as expressions. derivation: 'qty * price' instead of a hand-wired subscription.
  • Conditions are data. Show, hide, or require a field from other values, declared in the config.
  • Store it anywhere. A database row, an API response, a CMS field. Change the form without a redeploy.
